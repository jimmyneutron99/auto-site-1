I. Introduction & Market Overview (Approx. 350 words)

Edge computing is transforming industries, and its market size and growth are nothing short of remarkable. By 2025, the global edge computing market could be worth between $80 billion and $150 billion USD (Source: [Gartner](https://www.gartner.com/en/information-technology/market-researchers), IDC, Forrester). The compound annual growth rate is estimated to range from 15% to 25% between 2020 and 2025/2027 (Source: [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/) for recent research papers on the topic).
